What's up y'all, my name is Taylor and I'm a third year CS major here at Tech. Looking forward to getting to know everyone in bootcamp this semester :)

My main service experience is my time volunteering with AmeriCorps NCCC, a national service program like the Peace Corps but in the US and for shorter assignments. After spending a month in Sacramento, California, my team was sent off for three months in San Rafael, California helping with the city fire department's wildfire mitigation efforts. The next six months we aided the county health department's vaccine distribution in Salt Lake City, Utah. It was a huge growing experience and incredibly rewarding making an impact in communities that needed help.

Excited to join BoG and learn how to make an impact in a different way!
